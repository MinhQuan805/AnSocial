# Facebook Leads Tutorial

## Muc tieu
Lay lead submissions tu Facebook Lead Ads va day vao Notion.

## Pipeline de xuat
1. Tao request lay danh sach lead forms.
2. Lay lead items theo date range.
3. Chuan hoa key-value trong response.
4. Mapping field vao schema Notion.

## Field can co
- created_time
- form_id
- field_data
- ad_id
- campaign_id
